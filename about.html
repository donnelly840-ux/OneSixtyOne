About

161 is where it all started. The place where I figured out who I was, what I cared about, and what I wanted to make. I didn't know it at the time, but everything I do now started there — with too much time and just enough curiosity.

The agency came later. But the instinct was always the same — make things that mean something. Not just things that look good. Things that feel right.

We're a small, intentional team of strategists and creators who care about the work as much as the outcome. We don't take on everything. We take on the right things, and we go all in.

No templates. No shortcuts. No BS.
